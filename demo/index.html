Rules
1. Bet SOL on any outcome until kickoff. Betting locks at the scheduled kickoff time, enforced by the on-chain clock, not just this page.
2. The result is the final score including extra time. Level after 120 minutes = Draw. Penalty shootouts decide who advances, not the score, so they never decide the pot.
3. Settlement is trustless: anyone submits the score with a Merkle proof, verified on-chain against score fingerprints published by TxODDS. Wrong or forged scores cannot pass.
4. Every settlement has a 2 hour objection window. Anyone holding a proof with a later score timestamp can overwrite the standing one. After a quiet window, the market finalizes.
5. Winners split the entire pot in proportion to their stake. No fees.
6. If nobody backed the winning outcome, all bets are refundable in full. If only the winning outcome was backed, payouts equal stakes (same as a refund).
