Rules
1. Bet SOL on any outcome until kickoff. Betting locks at the scheduled kickoff time, enforced by the on-chain clock.
2. The result is the final score including extra time. Level after 120 minutes = Draw. Penalty shootouts decide who advances, not the score.
3. Settlement is trustless: anyone submits the score with a Merkle proof, verified on-chain against TxODDS's published fingerprints.
4. Every settlement has a 2 hour objection window; a proof with a later score timestamp always wins. Then the market finalizes.
5. Winners split the whole pot pro rata, no fees. If nobody backed the winner, all stakes refund in full.
